isorama

my portfolio website

isorama (from isometric & diorama) is the project title of my portfolio website. Throughout my learning of front-end frameworks, I will be consistently updating and improving how it was built. The end goal will be to have a 3D isometric diorama personal portfolio.


I was originally struggling with the colour palette of the website, but after scratching my head over it for longer than I'd like to admit, I looked over to my stack of tote bags and found the perfect combination.
